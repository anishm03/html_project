Creamy Pasta:

Ingredients:

  • 200gm Pasta
  • 1 Cup Cream
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Mix with cream and spices
  3. Serve hot

Cheese Pizza:

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pizza Dough
  • 1 Cup Pizza Sauce
  • 100gm Cheese

Steps:

  1. bake dough with sauce and veggies
  2. Add cheese
  3. Serve hot
